I'm a PhD student at the Oxford-Imperial CDT for Mathematics of Random Systems under the supervision of Cristopher Salvi, based at Imperial College London. I graduated from Università di Pisa in 2022 with a thesis written at ETH Zürich, under the supervision of Professor Josef Teichmann.

My interests lie in Rough Paths, Functional and Stochastic Analysis, Mathematical Finance, and Machine Learning. My research is now focused on the role of Signature Kernels in the setting of Neural CDEs.
