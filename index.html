Nicola Muça Cirone

Postgraduate researcher
Imperial College London

Email: n.muca-cirone22@imperial.ac.uk

I'm a PhD student at the Oxford-Imperial CDT for Mathematics of Random Systems under the supervision of Cristopher Salvi, based at Imperial College London.
I graduated from Università di Pisa in 2022 with a thesis written at ETH Zürich, under the supervision of Professor Josef Teichmann.

test My interests lie in Rough Paths, Functional and Stochastic Analysis, Mathematical Finance, and Machine Learning.
My research is now focused on understanding the scaling laws governing the behavior of randomly initialized Neural Networks both before and during training.
